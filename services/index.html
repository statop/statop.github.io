Services

Architectural Research

Peachtree Preservation offers a wide range of architectural history research and preservation planning services.  We provide services that address all aspects of historic preservation from National Register nominations, and local designations, to preservation planning, resource surveys, and preservation guidelines.                                                                                        

Genealogy Research

Peachtree Preservation offers genealogy research services. We create comprehensive family trees going back generations or can simply research a specific family member or branch.  All our research is tailored to our clients, and we will create a unique and specialized research protocol which meets the needs of our clients.

Historic Research

Peachtree Preservation is happy to conduct any other archival or historic research needed by our clients.  For example, we can conduct deed and title research, or obituary research.  We are happy to discuss your research needs.

Educational Programming

The researchers at Peachtree Preservation are always available to create specialized educational experiences for special interest groups with an interest in architecture, preservation, or genealogy.
